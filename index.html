What Penny does

Everything you need, nothing you don't

A full personal finance suite that works entirely on-device — no subscriptions, no data collection, no internet required.

💸

Transaction Tracking

Record income and expenses with categories, notes, multi-currency support, receipt scanning, reference numbers, and a full soft-delete audit trail.

📊

Budgets

Period-based budgets with per-category limits and live spending progress. Animated progress bars pulse amber at 80% and red when overspent.

🎯

Savings Goals

Set targets with deadlines, track contributions, and see the monthly savings amount required to hit your goal on time.

📈

Advanced Analytics

Spending forecasts (Holt-Winters + BG/NBD), cash flow charts, budget variance bars, category drill-downs, and what-if scenario sliders — all offline.

🤝

Expense Splitting

Create split groups, divide expenses equally or with custom percentages, track pairwise balances, and settle debts with a QR flow.

👥

Contacts & Associations

Find people, manage invitations, and keep shared finance activity organized with in-app notifications.

📷

Receipt Scanning

Scan receipts on device to capture transaction details quickly and keep them organized with your existing rules.

🌍

Multi-Currency

15+ currencies with live EC InforEuro exchange rates, offline gzip cache, and automatic base-currency conversion. Each transaction displays in its own currency.

📲

QR Code Sharing

Share transactions with QR codes that are quick to create, easy to scan, and work offline.

☁️

Cloud Sync & Backup

Optional cloud sync and backup keep your data available across devices when you choose to turn them on.

🔐

Secure Sync

Your synced data stays private and protected with account and device-level safeguards.

♿

Accessibility

5 text size presets, high-contrast theme (auto-activates from system setting), in-app reduce motion toggle, and colorblind-friendly directional icons on all amounts.

🔁

Recurring Transactions

Configurable templates with daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly recurrence. Auto-generated on app launch with smart scheduling.

🤖

AI Categorization

Rule-based auto-categorization with EXACT, CONTAINS, STARTS_WITH, and REGEX matching. Self-learns from your choices with up to 500 learned rules.

📄

Export & Reports

PDF financial reports, CSV/Excel exports, and full JSON backups of all 23 tables. Import CSV, Excel, and JSON with deduplication.

🏦

Apple Wallet Import

Import transactions from Apple Wallet on supported iPhone devices, with duplicate detection and easy review.

🔔

In-App Notifications

Helpful alerts for invites, activity, and budget updates appear inside the app when you need them.

🔒

Biometric Lock

Face ID, Touch ID, and PIN fallback help keep the app locked when you want extra protection.

Privacy by design

Offline-first, always.

Your data lives on your device. Penny is fully functional without any network connection. Cloud features are strictly opt-in — nothing leaves your device by default.

  • ✓No account required — all core features work on first launch
  • ✓Data stored locally on your device by default
  • ✓Private keys and sensitive data kept in protected app storage
  • ✓Optional cloud sync stays private and under your control
  • ✓Release builds connect only to the services they need
  • ✓Offline banner + connectivity guard on all network operations
  • ✓Receipt scanning runs on-device by default — no image data ever sent
  • ✓Complete data wipe on request — device and cloud data, under your control
