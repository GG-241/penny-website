How Penny handles your data and why the app is designed to collect as little as possible.

1 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Penny handles information when you use the app and related services. It covers the iOS app, optional cloud features, and any account-based functionality you choose to use.

3 Where Data Lives

By default, Penny stores financial data locally on your device. This includes transactions, budgets, goals, categories, app settings, and related local records. Core features do not require an account.

4 Optional Cloud Features

Some features may transmit data to service providers you choose to use. These include account sign-in, cloud sync, shared workspaces, purchase validation, and push notifications.

Apple Sign-In and Google Sign-In may share your name, email address, and related account identifiers depending on your settings. In-app purchases are processed by Apple and may expose purchase status, transaction identifiers, and receipt validation data to Penny so the app can unlock Premium features and restore purchases.

Receipt features may use camera or photo library access only when you choose to scan or attach a receipt. Location is optional and is never used for advertising or tracking.

5 Third-Party Services

Penny may use service providers for authentication, storage, push notifications, and platform-level purchase handling. Those providers may process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

6 Advertising and Brokering

Penny does not include third-party advertising, does not sell personal data, and does not broker your information. Your financial data is not used for ad targeting.

7 Security

Penny uses platform security features and encrypted transport for cloud features. Device authentication and signed sharing use cryptographic keys stored on the device.

8 Retention and Deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete it, delete the app, or reset the device. If you use account-based features, your cloud data is retained while the account is active and may be deleted when you delete the account, except where retention is required by law, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or financial record obligations.

You can delete local data in the app or by uninstalling Penny. You can request account deletion from Settings -> Account -> Delete Account. This is intended to remove account records and associated cloud data.

9 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, restrict, object to, or erase personal data. This includes the GDPR right to erasure. Many data actions are available directly in the app through export and deletion tools.

10 Children

Penny is not directed at children under 13 or the minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11 Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page.

12 Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact us through the official support channel associated with Penny or the project repository.
