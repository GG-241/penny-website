1 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the developer of Penny ("we", "us", or "the Licensor") handles information when you use the Penny personal finance application ("the Application"). It applies to all versions of the Application across Android, iOS, and Web platforms.

Penny is designed as an offline-first application. The core principle behind our data practices is simple: your financial data belongs to you, stays on your device, and is never transmitted unless you explicitly choose to use an optional cloud feature.

2 Data We Do Not Collect

By default, Penny does not collect, transmit, or store on any server:

  • Your financial transactions, budgets, savings goals, or expense records
  • Your bank account details or payment card numbers
  • Your name, email address, or phone number (unless you create an optional account)
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Contents of scanned receipts (OCR runs on-device via ML Kit by default)
  • Apple Wallet or FinanceKit transaction data (processed entirely on-device)
No account required: All core features — transactions, budgets, savings goals, analytics, receipt scanning, QR sharing, expense splitting, and export — work without creating an account or signing in.

4 Optional Cloud Features and Data Transmission

The following features are strictly opt-in and require your explicit action to enable. Each involves transmitting data from your device:

Google Drive Backup & Sync

  • When enabled, Penny creates encrypted backup files in your personal Google Drive
  • Data transmitted: a full database export (transactions, budgets, goals, categories, settings)
  • We do not have access to your Google Drive; backups are stored in your own account
  • Governed by Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

Supabase Cloud Sync (Account Required)

  • When you create an account (via Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In), your data can be synced to a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database
  • Data transmitted: transactions, budgets, goals, workspace data, contact associations, and user profile information
  • Used to enable collaborative workspaces, real-time sync, contacts and associations, and push notifications
  • Data is protected by PostgreSQL Row Level Security (RLS) policies — you can only access your own data

Self-Hosted OCR Backend (Optional)

  • If configured, receipt images may be sent to a self-hosted PaddleOCR or docTR server for text extraction
  • The server address is configured by you; we do not operate or have access to your self-hosted backend
  • By default, receipt scanning uses on-device ML Kit and no images are transmitted

Exchange Rate Updates

  • Penny periodically fetches exchange rate data from the European Commission's EC InforEuro service
  • This request transmits only standard HTTP headers (no personal or financial data)
  • Rates are cached locally for offline use

6 Advertising

The free tier of Penny displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information, advertising identifiers, and usage data to serve personalized or contextual ads in accordance with Google's advertising policies.

Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience — no ads are displayed and no advertising-related data is collected or transmitted.

You can manage ad personalization through your device's privacy settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

7 Analytics and Crash Reporting

Penny may use anonymized, aggregated analytics to understand app stability and usage patterns. No personally identifiable financial data is included in analytics or crash reports.

Crash reports, if collected, may include device model, operating system version, app version, and stack trace information to help diagnose and fix bugs.

8 Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Local encryption: Cryptographic private keys (ECDSA P-256) are stored in FlutterSecureStorage, which uses the platform's secure enclave (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android)
  • Row Level Security: When using Supabase cloud sync, all database access is governed by PostgreSQL RLS policies that ensure users can only access their own data
  • Signed sharing: QR code transaction sharing uses ECDSA P-256 digital signatures with 24-hour expiry to prevent tampering
  • Release build guards: Release builds of the Application are configured to never communicate with development or localhost endpoints
  • Transport security: All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and any account credentials.

9 Data Retention and Deletion

Local data: All data stored on your device remains there until you delete it within the app or uninstall the Application. We have no access to or control over locally stored data.

Cloud data (Supabase): If you use cloud sync, your data is retained on Supabase servers for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time through the Application's settings.

Google Drive backups: Backup files in your Google Drive are under your control. You can delete them at any time through Google Drive.

Account deletion: Upon account deletion, all data associated with your account on our servers is permanently deleted. This includes profile information, synced transactions, workspace memberships, and contact associations. This process is irreversible.

10 Children's Privacy

Penny is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data through an optional cloud feature, please contact us so we can delete it.

11 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you (applicable only if you use cloud features)
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data at any time using the built-in CSV, Excel, or JSON export features
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data for advertising purposes via your device settings

For locally stored data, you have full control at all times — you can view, edit, export, and delete your data directly within the Application without contacting us.

Data portability built in: Penny supports full JSON backup of all 19 database tables, plus CSV and Excel export of transactions and PDF financial reports. You always own your data and can take it with you.
